Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, located in Paris, France, was completed in 1889 and is one of the most recognizable structures in the world. Designed by Gustave Eiffel, it stands at 330 meters (1,083 feet) tall.

Great Wall

The Great Wall of China stretches over 13,000 miles and was built to protect Chinese states from invasions. Construction started as early as the 7th century BC and continued for centuries.

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu, located in Peru, is an ancient Incan city built in the 15th century. It is one of the most famous archaeological sites in the world and attracts millions of visitors annually.

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty, gifted by France to the United States, stands on Liberty Island in New York Harbor. It was dedicated on October 28, 1886, and is a symbol of freedom and democracy.
